about neptro

neptro is comprised of a small group of Nepalis living in the U.S. that share a common curiosity about how emerging technologies and enterprises are shaping markets back in Nepal. This is our way of educating ourselves, and in the process others, about Nepali industries and the people shaping them.

We are researching and writing articles in our free time regarding topics that we find interesting. Please feel free to reach out to us with any feedback or comments. We appreciate anyone that takes the time to read our content.
