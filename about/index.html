about us

neptro is an open network for nepali entrepreneurs providing research on emerging trends and technologies along with sharing their stories and experiences.

neptro strives to share the stories of innovative individuals with a nepali heritage that are developing unique businesses all over the world. neptro aims to showcase their achievements and experiences and bring like minded individuals close together in the process.

The Team

The neptro team is composed of group of curious Nepalis with entrepreneurial ambitions that value the immense power of representation. Thus we have set out to find ambitious Nepalis who are doing innovative work that deserves to be in the lime light.
